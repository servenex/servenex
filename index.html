Visa-on-Arrival destinations for Indians


For Indian passport holders, Visa-on-Arrival destinations provide a convenient and hassle-free way to travel abroad without the need for pre-arranged visas.

Popular destinations like Thailand, the Maldives, Cambodia, and Kenya allow Indian travelers to obtain their visa upon arrival, simplifying the travel process. Typically, you will need a valid passport, a return or onward ticket, and proof of sufficient funds.

Visa-on-Arrival options are available in other popular destinations such as Sri Lanka, Indonesia, and Seychelles, making these locations ideal for vacations, business trips, or cultural explorations.

No-Visa Travel Countries


For Indian passport holders, No-Visa Travel countries offer the ultimate convenience, allowing hassle-free entry without the need for a visa.

Popular destinations like Malaysia, Nepal, Bhutan, the Maldives, Mauritius, Indonesia, Fiji, and Haiti offer visa-free travel for Indian travelers, making it easier to explore these beautiful countries without any pre-arranged visa requirements—perfect for spontaneous vacations or business trips.

While these countries offer visa-free entry, it is important to check specific entry conditions such as passport validity or proof of onward travel.

Schengen Visa


For travelers planning to explore Europe, the Schengen Visa offers the convenience of visiting multiple countries with one visa.

This visa allows you to travel freely within the 27 Schengen Area countries, including popular destinations like France, Germany, Italy, and Spain, without the need for separate visas for each.

Whether you're planning a short vacation, business trip, or a cultural exploration, the Schengen Visa simplifies the process, offering flexibility and ease of travel across Europe.
